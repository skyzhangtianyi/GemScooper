Our Mission

We handle the mess,
so you can share the love.

Tucson is meant to be enjoyed outdoors. It's about slow mornings, golden sunsets, and the moments your family will actually remember. Worrying about the dog's mess shouldn't be one of them.

We believe that cleaning the yard is a chore that steals time and energy away from what matters most. As fellow dog owners, we built Gem Scooper to handle the dirty work so you don't have to. We operate quietly, thoroughly, and on a seamless schedule — a premium service delivered by people who truly love dogs.

We keep the yard immaculate. We keep the dogs happy. And you get your weekends back.

Our Service

Professional pet waste removal.

We're a dedicated dog waste removal service for the homes of Tucson. One service, done thoroughly — every visit, every yard, every time.

  • i. Trained crew, professional equipment. We handle the unpleasant part — gloves, sanitized tools, sealed disposal — so you never have to.
  • ii. Weekly, bi-weekly, or one-time visits. No time limits. We stay until every corner of your yard is genuinely clean — not "good enough."
  • iii. Quiet, no-contact service. We never enter your home. Side gate access, locked behind us, in and out without disturbing the household.
  • iv. Health observations along the way. While we work, we watch — for changes in stool that may signal a health concern. We let you know.
  • v. Yard awareness reports. Pests, wildlife signs, fence damage — anything we notice in the yard, we report so you can act early.
